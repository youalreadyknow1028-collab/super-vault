Demolition Methodologies and Cost Considerations

• Total Building Demolition- discussed various methods, including removing ACM sheetrock, then roofing, before tearing down the buildina to then remove the foundation walls.

• This would involve setting up decon showers and multiple stages of air testing.

• Alternate approach would have the Village condemn the building and remove all material as contaminated waste.

• This should allow a shorter demolition schedule, but may result in a slightly higher cost for the additional contaminated material being disposed
