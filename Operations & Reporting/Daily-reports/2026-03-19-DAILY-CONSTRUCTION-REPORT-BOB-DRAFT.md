## 📋 IMPORTANT DISCOVERY — Rock Chipping Permit Issue (Mamaroneck Parks)

**Background:**
Today we analyzed the email thread and spec book regarding Mike Perri's (Town) request for a rock chipping permit. We made a critical discovery from **Plan C-3 General Notes** (verified from actual drawing):

**Plan C-3 Note 3 — VERIFIED:**
> "TEST BORING (B-) LOCATIONS APPROXIMATELY SHOWN IN ACCORDANCE WITH GEOTECHNICAL REPORT PREPARED BY CARLIN-SIMPSON & ASSOCIATES DATED JUNE 5, 2023"

**Plan C-3 Note 5 — VERIFIED:**
> "APPROXIMATELY 50 CUBIC YARDS OF ROCK REMOVAL IS ANTICIPATED DURING THE COURSE OF EXCAVATION AND SITEWORK ASSOCIATED WITH THE PROJECT"

**Key Finding:**
The Town DID disclose approximately 50 cubic yards of rock removal in Note 5. However, the **method of removal** (rock chipping vs. standard excavation) and **associated permit requirements** are NOT in the Demolition specification (Section 02 41 00).

**Your Position:**
While the Town disclosed the existence of rock (50 CY in Note 5), rock CHIPPING (specialized equipment/permits) was not reasonably anticipated in the demolition scope. If actual conditions require chipping vs. standard excavation, or if rock exceeds 50 CY, this constitutes a changed condition.

**Recommendation:** Request Mike Perri confirm that the 50 CY disclosed in Note 5 can be removed with standard excavation equipment. If chipping is required due to differing conditions, this should be a Change Order or separate contract.
