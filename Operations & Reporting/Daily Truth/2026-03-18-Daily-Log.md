## Yorktown Site Meeting – Richie (Spruce Contracting)

- Met Richie on site—reviewed full plan:
  - Break room: immediate demo (walls, kitchen, lockers, furniture).
  - Bunk room: finish blocked until Neighbor Electric rough-in complete.
- Furring wall in bunk room: drawings specify 7/8" hat channel, but ignore existing bump-out at bottom—recommend 3-5/8" studs to clear obstruction.
- Richie orders material for Friday delivery; crew starts tomorrow with on-hand stock.
- Drawings issue (David Tetro): demo plan omits existing kitchen and walls in bunk room—assumes they're not there. These must be removed to match reflected plan. Notified Bob—recommend contacting David Tetro ASAP for direction or change order.
- Break room walls: wood framing, no insulation—full demo needed before new framing/insulation/ceiling.
- Existing electric: all surface-mount conduit; demo drawings silent on removal.
- Bunk room plan: only switches shown—no receptacles. Existing receptacles, conduit, and junction boxes unaddressed.
- NEC 314.29(A): can't hide junction boxes behind drywall, plaster, or permanent ceiling—strictly prohibited.
- Recommendation: frame new walls in front of existing conduit/boxes, pull everything up above suspended ceiling, add new boxes, then run new receptacles, switches, and lighting from there.
- Yorktown bringing in own electrician—adds coordination risk; need clear plan before framing.
